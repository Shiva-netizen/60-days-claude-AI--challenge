Nutrition Sources & Methodology
Transparency about how NutriScope calculates your targets
Data Sources
ICMR Indian Council of Medical Research — Recommended Dietary Allowances (RDA) for India, 2020
NIN National Institute of Nutrition — Nutritive Value of Indian Foods
USDA USDA FoodData Central — Nutritional composition database
WHO World Health Organization — Nutrient requirements and dietary guidelines
NIH National Institutes of Health — Dietary Reference Intakes (DRI)
Calculation Methods
BMR: Mifflin-St Jeor equation
TDEE: BMR × Physical Activity Level (PAL)
Protein: 0.8–1.2g per kg body weight
Carbs: 45–65% of total calories
Fat: 20–35% of total calories
Fibre: 14g per 1000 kcal
