# Day 13 - AI-Powered Job Discovery with Claude Connectors

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude Connectors can integrate external platforms such as Indeed and other job sources to automate job discovery, role matching, market analysis, and career planning.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Modern job searching often involves:

* Searching multiple job portals
* Filtering irrelevant opportunities
* Comparing job descriptions
* Identifying skill gaps
* Tracking market trends

Claude Connectors simplify this process by connecting external job platforms directly to Claude and allowing AI to perform intelligent job discovery and analysis.

---

# 🔗 What are Claude Connectors?

Claude Connectors enable Claude to access external information sources and use them for personalized analysis.

Instead of manually searching through hundreds of jobs, Claude can:

* Search opportunities
* Analyze job requirements
* Compare roles against your profile
* Prioritize opportunities
* Identify skill gaps
