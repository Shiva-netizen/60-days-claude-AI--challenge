# Day 14 - AI Job Red Flag Detector

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can help job seekers evaluate opportunities before applying by identifying hidden risks, misleading job descriptions, toxic workplace signals, remote work concerns, and hiring red flags.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Job applications are a two-way evaluation process.

Most candidates focus on:

* Resume Optimization
* Interview Preparation
* Networking
* Job Applications

However, evaluating the company and role is equally important.

This project demonstrates how Claude can act as an AI-powered Job Red Flag Detector to help candidates make smarter career decisions.

---

# 🔍 Why Job Analysis Matters

Not every job posting represents a healthy opportunity.

Some listings may contain:

* Unrealistic expectations
* Burnout indicators
* Misleading remote policies
* Poor compensation transparency
* High turnover signals

Claude can analyze job descriptions and company information to uncover these risks before investing time in the application process.

---

# 🛠 AI Red Flag Detection Framework

The system evaluates opportunities across five major categories.

---

# 1️⃣ Unrealistic Requirements

Claude identifies situations where companies expect too much from candidates.

### Common Signals

* Excessive years of experience for junior roles
* Multiple unrelated responsibilities
* Unrealistic technology stacks
* Contradictory expectations

---

### Example

Entry-Level Position

Requirements:

* 5+ years experience
* Full-stack development
* Cloud engineering
* Machine learning
* DevOps

Potential Risk:

⚠️ Role expectations may not align with title or compensation.

---

# 2️⃣ Toxic Workplace Signals

Claude scans language patterns associated with unhealthy work cultures.

---

### Common Keywords

* Rockstar
* Ninja
* Hustler
* Work Hard Play Hard
* Wear Many Hats
* Fast-Paced Environment
* Highly Ambitious Team

---

### Possible Risks

* Long working hours
* Poor work-life balance
* Burnout culture
* Undefined responsibilities

---

### Example

"We are looking for a rockstar willing to go above and beyond."

Potential Risk:

⚠️ Possible expectation of excessive overtime.

---

# 3️⃣ Remote Job Authenticity

Many jobs advertise remote work but include hidden restrictions.

Claude identifies:

### Hidden Office Requirements

Examples:

* Occasional office visits
* Hybrid expectations
* Onsite onboarding

---

### Relocation Risks

Examples:

* Future relocation requirements
* Location restrictions

---

### Misleading Remote Claims

Example:

"Remote"

But:

Must live within commuting distance.

Potential Risk:

⚠️ Not truly remote.

---

# 4️⃣ Hiring Risks

Claude evaluates the hiring process itself.

---

### Missing Salary Information

Risk:

Lack of compensation transparency.

---

### Vague Responsibilities

Risk:

Role scope may be unclear.

---

### Excessive Qualification Requirements

Risk:

Unrealistic expectations.

---

### Suspicious Hiring Practices

Examples:

* Unpaid projects
* Multiple interview rounds without clarity
* Unclear evaluation criteria

---

# 5️⃣ Company Risks

Claude analyzes company-related concerns.

---

### Reputation Issues

Examples:

* Negative employee reviews
* Leadership concerns
* Ethical controversies

---

### Stability Risks

Examples:

* Financial uncertainty
* Frequent restructuring
* High employee turnover

---

### Layoff Indicators

Examples:

* Recent layoffs
* Hiring freezes
* Rapid downsizing

---

# 📊 Risk Assessment Framework

The system generates:

## Overall Risk Score

Range:

0–100

### Interpretation

0–25

🟢 Low Risk

---

26–50

🟡 Moderate Risk

---

51–75

🟠 High Risk

---

76–100

🔴 Severe Risk

---

# 🚩 Top Red Flags

Each issue is scored by severity.

Scale:

1–10

Examples:

| Red Flag                 | Severity |
| ------------------------ | -------- |
| Missing Salary Range     | 6        |
| Excessive Requirements   | 8        |
| Misleading Remote Policy | 9        |
| Burnout Indicators       | 7        |

# 🎯 Final Verdict

Claude provides one of three recommendations:

### ✅ Apply

Strong opportunity with minimal concerns.

---

### ⚠️ Apply with Caution

Opportunity has some risks requiring validation.

---

### ❌ Avoid

Significant concerns identified.

# 🎤 Smart Interview Questions

One of the most valuable outputs.

Claude generates questions designed to validate risks.

Examples:

### Work-Life Balance

How does the team manage workload during busy periods?

---

### Career Growth

What does success look like in the first six months?

---

### Remote Policy

Can you clarify expectations around office attendance?

---

### Team Stability

What is the average tenure of team members?

---

### Performance Expectations

How are priorities managed when multiple urgent projects arise?
