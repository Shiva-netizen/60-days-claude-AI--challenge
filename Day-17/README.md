# 🚀 Project Overview

Data analysts spend a significant amount of time:

* Cleaning data
* Calculating KPIs
* Building dashboards
* Creating visualizations
* Generating business insights

This project demonstrates how Claude can automate the entire analytics workflow by transforming raw vehicle fuel and maintenance data into a complete interactive dashboard.

# 📈 KPIs Calculated

## Average Cost per KM

Formula:

Fuel Cost ÷ Distance

Measures:

Vehicle operating efficiency.

---

## Average CO₂ per KM

Formula:

CO₂ Emitted ÷ Distance

Measures:

Environmental impact.

---

## Average Maintenance Cost per KM

Formula:

Maintenance Cost ÷ Distance

Measures:

Long-term ownership costs.

---

## Average Refuel / Recharge Time

Measures:

Operational convenience.

One of the most interesting business calculations.

## Pump Saving

Measures fuel price savings compared with Petrol.

---

## Running Penalty

Measures efficiency differences.

---

## Break-Even Price

Identifies the fuel price point where E85 becomes economically comparable.

---

## E85 Score

Calculated using:

### Cost

4 Points

### CO₂

3 Points

### Refueling

2 Points

### Maintenance

1 Point

Total:

10 Points
