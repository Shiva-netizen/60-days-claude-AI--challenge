# Day 18 - Building a Brain Dump Action Planner with Claude Skills

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude Custom Skills can transform messy notes, meeting transcripts, brainstorming sessions, voice memos, and stream-of-consciousness thoughts into structured action plans, project dashboards, decision logs, and task management systems.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

One of the biggest productivity challenges is information overload.

Ideas come from:

* Meetings
* Brainstorming Sessions
* Voice Notes
* Project Discussions
* Personal Notes
* Team Conversations

Most information remains unorganized and difficult to act upon.

This project demonstrates how Claude can automatically convert unstructured information into organized project dashboards and actionable workflows.

---

# 🧠 What is the Brain Dump Action Planner?

The Brain Dump Action Planner is a reusable Claude Skill designed to process raw information and organize it into structured outputs.

Instead of manually sorting through notes, Claude automatically identifies:

* Key Takeaways
* Action Items
* Open Questions
* Risks
* Conflicts
* Decisions
* Follow-Ups

# 📋 Dashboard Sections

The skill automatically creates a structured dashboard.

---

## Summary

Provides a concise overview of:

* Meeting
* Transcript
* Brain Dump
* Notes

Purpose:

Quick understanding of the content.

---

## Key Takeaways

Displays important insights as:

* Cards
* Highlights
* Priority Items

---

## Action Items

One of the most important sections.

Displays:

| Task | Owner | Deadline | Status |
| ---- | ----- | -------- | ------ |

If information is missing:

Not Specified

No assumptions are made.

---

## Open Questions

Tracks:

* Pending Clarifications
* Unresolved Decisions
* Missing Information

Uses:

❓ Open Question

Indicators.

---

## Risks & Blockers

Highlights:

* Dependencies
* Risks
* Constraints
* Delays

Priority badges are automatically applied.

---

## Conflicts

Detects conflicting information such as:

* Multiple Deadlines
* Conflicting Decisions
* Ownership Conflicts
* Resource Conflicts

Uses:

⚠️ Conflict

Indicators.

---

## Additional Notes

Stores information that does not fit into other categories.

Ensures no important context is lost.

---

# 📊 Status Management System

The dashboard uses visual indicators for prioritization.

---

## Priority Levels

🔴 High Priority

Critical tasks requiring immediate attention.

---

🟠 Medium Priority

Important but not urgent.

---

🟢 Low Priority

Routine tasks.

---

## Task Status

✅ Completed

Finished items.

---

⏳ Pending

Waiting for completion.

---

❓ Open Question

Needs clarification.

---

⚠️ Conflict

Conflicting information detected.

# 🎙 Transcript Mode

Special workflow for meeting transcripts.

Additional features include:

---

## Speaker Summary

Summarizes contributions from each speaker.

---

## Decisions by Speaker

Tracks decisions associated with individuals.

---

## Action Items by Speaker

Groups tasks based on ownership.

---

## Attribution Notes

Highlights unclear ownership or responsibility.

Uses speaker names exactly as provided.

---

# 🔀 Merge Mode

Designed for combining multiple sources.

Examples:

* Multiple Meeting Notes
* Several Brain Dumps
* Project Updates
* Team Discussions

---

## Duplicate Items Section

Identifies repeated information.

---

## Conflict Review Section

Highlights contradictory information.

---

## Source Tracking

Displays the origin of each note.

No automatic conflict resolution occurs.
