# Day 24 - AI Startup Business Viability & Growth Strategy Advisor

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can evaluate whether a startup has the potential to become a sustainable business by analyzing revenue models, customer acquisition strategies, competitive advantages, growth risks, and investment readiness.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Validating a problem and building an MVP are important milestones.

However, successful startups must also answer critical business questions:

* Will customers pay consistently?
* How will users discover the product?
* Can customer acquisition scale?
* Does the business have defensible advantages?
* Can the company become profitable?
* Is the startup attractive to investors?

This project demonstrates how Claude can act as an AI Co-Founder, Growth Strategist, YC Advisor, and Business Consultant to evaluate startup sustainability.
