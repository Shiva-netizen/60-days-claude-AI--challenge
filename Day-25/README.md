# Day 25 - AI Shark Tank Simulator

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can generate complete business simulation applications that combine startup evaluation, investor psychology, scoring systems, interactive workflows, and modern web interfaces to simulate a real investor pitch experience.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Pitching a startup is more than presenting an idea.

Founders must convince investors that their business has:

* A real customer problem
* A scalable solution
* A sustainable business model
* Strong execution capability
* Investment potential

This project demonstrates how Claude can generate a complete AI Shark Tank Simulator that evaluates startup ideas through multiple investor perspectives.

# 🦈 AI Shark Panel

Four AI judges evaluate the startup from different perspectives.

---

## Venture Capitalist

Primary Focus:

* Market Size
* Scalability
* Growth Potential
* Venture Returns

Questions include:

* Is the market large enough?
* Can this business scale globally?

---

## Founder

Primary Focus:

* Execution
* Team Capability
* Product Development
* Problem-Solving

Questions include:

* Can the team build this?
* Why are you the right founder?

---

## Customer

Primary Focus:

* Real Customer Value
* Product Usability
* Pain Point Validation

Questions include:

* Why would customers switch?
* Does this solve an urgent problem?

---

## Angel Investor

Primary Focus:

* Revenue
* Profitability
* Unit Economics
* Financial Sustainability

Questions include:

* How will the business make money?
* What are the biggest financial risks?

---

# 🎤 Interactive Pitch Round

Each AI judge asks two personalized questions.

The founder submits responses, and judges provide dynamic reactions based on the quality of the answers.

This creates a realistic investor-founder conversation rather than a static evaluation.

---

# 📊 Startup Scorecard

Every startup is evaluated across five dimensions.

| Category              | Score |
| --------------------- | ----- |
| Market Potential      | 0–100 |
| Innovation            | 0–100 |
| Business Model        | 0–100 |
| Execution             | 0–100 |
| Investment Worthiness | 0–100 |

Each score includes a short explanation to help founders understand strengths and improvement areas.

---

# 💰 Investment Decision

After evaluation, the simulator generates one of four outcomes.

### 🟢 Invest

The startup demonstrates strong market potential, execution, and business fundamentals.

---

### 🟡 Come Back Later

The concept is promising but requires more validation or traction.

---

### 🟠 Acquire

The technology or product has value, but it may fit better within an existing company.

---

### 🔴 Reject

The current opportunity lacks sufficient validation or investment readiness.
