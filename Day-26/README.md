# 🚀 Project Overview

Healthcare operations involve many interconnected processes that can be difficult to understand through static diagrams or documentation.

One of the most important workflows is **Prior Authorization (PA)**—a process where healthcare providers obtain approval from insurance companies before certain treatments, procedures, or medications are covered.

This project demonstrates how Claude can generate a fully interactive Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator that teaches this process through hands-on interaction.

---

# 🏥 What is Prior Authorization?

Prior Authorization is a review process used by insurance companies to determine whether a requested medical service is medically necessary before approving payment.

It helps ensure that:

* Appropriate treatments are provided
* Clinical guidelines are followed
* Healthcare costs are managed
* Patients receive eligible coverage

# 👥 Three Interactive Workflow Lanes

The simulator visually separates responsibilities across three participants.

---

## 🧑 Patient

Responsibilities include:

* Seeking treatment
* Providing medical history
* Waiting for authorization
* Receiving final outcome

---

## 🏥 Provider

Responsible for:

* Clinical evaluation
* Documentation
* Medical necessity review
* Prior Authorization submission
* Appeals when required

---

## 🏢 Payer (Insurance)

Responsible for:

* Reviewing submitted documents
* Evaluating medical necessity
* Approving or denying requests
* Requesting additional information
* Conducting peer-to-peer reviews
