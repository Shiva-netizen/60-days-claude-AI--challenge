# Day 27 - AI Prior Authorization Story Simulator

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can generate complete educational applications that combine storytelling, conversational interfaces, branching decisions, and healthcare concepts to teach complex insurance workflows through interactive learning.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Healthcare processes are often explained using lengthy documents, flowcharts, or policy manuals.

While informative, these formats can be difficult for patients and beginners to understand.

This project demonstrates how Claude can transform the Prior Authorization process into an interactive story where users learn by following conversations, making decisions, and seeing the consequences of each choice.

---

# 🏥 What is Prior Authorization?

Prior Authorization (PA) is a healthcare review process where an insurance company evaluates whether a prescribed treatment, procedure, or medication meets coverage requirements before approving payment.

Rather than reading policy documents, users experience the process through realistic conversations between patients and healthcare professionals.

# 📖 Story Progression

## Scene 1 — Doctor Visit

Rahul visits **City Medical Center** with persistent joint pain.

Dr. Patel diagnoses **Rheumatoid Arthritis** and recommends **Humira** as the appropriate treatment.

Users learn why some specialty medications require insurance authorization before treatment begins.

---

## Scene 2 — Insurance Roadblock

Dr. Patel's office submits a Prior Authorization request directly to **StarCare Health** *(illustrative example)*.

Workflow:

Provider → PA Request → Payer

The story explains that approved authorizations remain on file, preventing unnecessary repeat requests for the same medication.

---

## Scene 3 — What is Prior Authorization?

Priya explains:

* Why Prior Authorization exists
* Medical necessity review
* Step therapy requirements
* Why delays can affect chronic diseases

Educational Insight:

> AMA 2023 Prior Authorization Survey:
> Prior Authorization causes treatment delays in the majority of cases.

Users understand both the insurer's perspective and the patient's experience.

---

## Scene 4 — Insurance Review

Priya walks through how the payer evaluates the request.

Checks include:

* Eligibility Verification
* Clinical Documentation
* ICD-10 Diagnosis Matching
* Step Therapy History

Each requirement is explained in plain language so beginners understand why insurers request supporting documentation.

---

## Scene 5 — Initial Denial

The request is denied because step therapy documentation is incomplete.

The application emphasizes:

A denial is **not** the end of the process.

Additional educational insight:

> Resolving Prior Authorization denials can require more than two staff hours for physician offices.

Users see the operational impact on healthcare providers.

---

## Scene 6 — Appeal Process

Rahul and Dr. Patel work together to prepare the appeal.

Required documents include:

* Updated Clinical Records
* Step Therapy Documentation
* Letter of Medical Necessity
* Formal Appeal Submission

The simulator explains how appeals strengthen the clinical justification.

---

## Scene 7 — Approval

StarCare Health approves the Prior Authorization.

The application displays:

* Approval Notification
* Authorization Reference Number
* Permanent Approval Record

The story explains that future Humira prescriptions generally do not require repeating the entire authorization process.

---

## Scene 8 — Final Takeaways

### Patient Perspective

Users understand both patient experiences and healthcare operations.

# 🎮 Interactive Decision System

* Ask another question
* Continue to the next step

Each decision influences dialogue while maintaining the overall educational pathway.
