# Day 29 - Operation Lifeline: AI Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can generate complete enterprise simulations that teach supply chain strategy, crisis management, executive decision-making, supplier negotiations, and AI-powered operations through an interactive React application.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Modern supply chains are complex ecosystems that connect suppliers, factories, warehouses, logistics providers, and customers across multiple countries.

Unexpected disruptions such as factory fires, supplier failures, cyberattacks, or shipping delays can impact revenue, customer satisfaction, and business continuity.

This project demonstrates how Claude can generate a complete business simulation where players take the role of a Supply Chain Leader responsible for guiding an organization through a major operational crisis.

---

# 🌍 What is Supply Chain Management?

Supply Chain Management (SCM) is the coordination of people, processes, inventory, suppliers, logistics, and technology to deliver products efficiently while balancing cost, quality, speed, and customer satisfaction.

Successful organizations continuously manage risks while ensuring products reach customers on time.

# 🚨 Crisis Generator

Claude randomly creates one high-impact business disruption.

Possible scenarios include:

* 🔥 Factory Fire
* 💸 Supplier Bankruptcy
* 🚢 Port Strike
* 💻 Cyberattack
* 🌊 Flood
* 🏭 Raw Material Shortage
* 🌍 Political Conflict

# 🛡 Supply Chain War Room

Players are presented with six possible response actions.

Examples include:

* Activate Backup Supplier
* Expedite Shipments
* Increase Safety Stock
* Reallocate Inventory
* Prioritize High-Value Customers
* Delay Non-Critical Orders

The player selects three actions.

The simulator then updates key business metrics with animated progress bars.

Metrics include:

* Cost
* Inventory
* Profit
* Delivery Speed
* Customer Satisfaction

Each decision creates different operational outcomes.
