# Day 30 - AI Supply Chain Builder

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can generate complete enterprise simulations that teach supply chain design, optimization, and business trade-offs through interactive decision-making and hands-on learning.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

A successful supply chain isn't built by chance—it is carefully designed.

Every decision, from selecting suppliers to choosing transportation and inventory strategies, affects cost, delivery speed, customer satisfaction, sustainability, and operational risk.

This project demonstrates how Claude can generate an interactive Supply Chain Builder where users design a complete supply chain while learning the business impact of every decision.

---

# 🌍 What is a Supply Chain?

A supply chain is the network of people, facilities, suppliers, warehouses, transportation systems, and technology that work together to deliver products from raw materials to customers.

A well-designed supply chain helps businesses:

* Deliver products faster
* Reduce operational costs
* Improve customer satisfaction
* Minimize risks
* Build long-term resilience

# 🤝 Supplier Strategy

The first major decision is supplier selection.

### Single Supplier

Benefits:

* Lower management complexity
* Better pricing through volume

Trade-Offs:

* Higher disruption risk
* Less flexibility

---

### Multiple Suppliers

Benefits:

* Greater resilience
* Reduced dependency
* Improved continuity

Trade-Offs:

* More coordination
* Potentially higher costs

Before making a choice, the simulator explains what supplier diversification means and why it matters.
