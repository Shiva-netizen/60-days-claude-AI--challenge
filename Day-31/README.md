# 🚀 Project Overview

Global supply chains never stop moving.

Every minute, operations teams monitor suppliers, warehouses, transportation networks, factories, and customer orders to keep products flowing efficiently.

Unexpected disruptions—such as supplier delays, port congestion, weather events, or equipment failures—can quickly impact costs, service levels, and customer satisfaction.

This project demonstrates how Claude can generate a fully interactive **AI Supply Chain Control Tower**, placing users in the role of **Head of Operations** responsible for managing a live stream of operational challenges.

---

# 🌍 What is a Supply Chain Control Tower?

A Supply Chain Control Tower is a centralized operations hub that provides real-time visibility across logistics, inventory, suppliers, transportation, and production.

Its purpose is to:

* Monitor operations continuously
* Detect disruptions early
* Prioritize critical issues
* Improve decision-making
* Protect customer commitments
* Reduce operational risk

# 🚨 Operational Alert Center

The dashboard continuously generates randomized business disruptions.

Possible scenarios include:

* 🚢 Port Congestion
* 🚛 Supplier Delay
* 🚚 Truck Breakdown
* 📦 Warehouse Stockout
* 🛃 Customs Inspection
* 📈 Demand Spike
* 🏭 Factory Machine Failure
* 🌪 Weather Disruption
* 📊 Inventory Count Error
* 📦 Damaged Shipment

Each alert contains:

* Alert Title
* Business Description
* Priority Level
* Time Remaining
* Operational Impact

---

# ⚡ Response Actions

For every alert, users choose how to respond.

Available actions include:

* Expedite Shipment
* Use Backup Supplier
* Reroute Trucks
* Increase Production
* Transfer Inventory
* Approve Air Freight
* Ignore
* Delay Decision

Each option carries unique trade-offs that influence short-term and long-term business outcomes.

# 🎮 Decision Engine

### Poor Decisions
