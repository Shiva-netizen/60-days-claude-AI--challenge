# Day 34 - Marketing Detective

## 🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can generate immersive educational applications that teach marketing strategy through detective-style investigations. Analyze campaign evidence, uncover hidden marketing mistakes, interpret customer behavior, and make data-driven decisions through interactive gameplay.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Great marketers don't rely on guesswork.

They collect evidence, analyze customer behavior, identify patterns, and continuously optimize campaigns based on data.

This project demonstrates how Claude can generate an interactive detective game where users investigate fictional marketing campaigns, uncover hidden problems, and recommend strategic improvements before revealing the final solution.

---

# 🕵️ What is Marketing Analysis?

Marketing analysis is the process of evaluating campaign performance, customer behavior, channel effectiveness, and business outcomes to understand why a campaign succeeded or failed.

A good marketer asks questions such as:

* What happened?
* Why did it happen?
* What evidence supports that conclusion?
* What should be improved next?
