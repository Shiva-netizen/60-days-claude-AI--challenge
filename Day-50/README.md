Day 50 – 🛡️ Defend Your Experience
🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can transform resumes, portfolios, research papers, startup stories, and professional experiences into adaptive interview simulations that help users confidently defend every claim they make.

🚀 Project Overview

Most interview preparation tools focus on improving resumes.

This project takes a completely different approach.

Instead of rewriting your experience, Claude acts as an intelligent skeptic, extracts every meaningful claim from your uploaded documents, challenges each one with progressively deeper questions, and helps you build evidence-backed answers before facing a real interviewer.

🛡️ What is Defend Your Experience?

Defend Your Experience is an AI-powered interview preparation platform that transforms personal documents into realistic, adaptive interview conversations.

Rather than asking generic interview questions, the application analyzes the user's own experience and generates personalized follow-up questions that evolve based on previous responses.

The platform supports:

Resume Defense
LinkedIn Validation
Portfolio Interviews
Startup Story Defense
Research Presentation Practice
Freelance Project Interviews
Performance Review Preparation
Personal Brand Validation
