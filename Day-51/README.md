Day 51 – 🚀 Product Discovery & Sprint Planning
🎯 Objective

Learn how Claude AI can act as a product mentor, co-founder, and technical planning partner by guiding you from an initial idea to a realistic, ship-ready product roadmap through structured product discovery, scope definition, and sprint planning.

🚀 Project Overview

Building successful software starts long before writing code.

This project demonstrates how Claude can interview founders, refine ideas, eliminate scope creep, validate product direction, and produce a professional Product Requirements Document (PRD), implementation blueprint, and pitch deck that become the single source of truth for a complete 10-day product sprint.

💡 What is Product Discovery & Sprint Planning?

Product Discovery & Sprint Planning is an AI-powered product strategy platform that helps creators move from "I have an idea" to a clearly defined, achievable product plan.

Rather than jumping directly into development, the platform conducts structured interviews, challenges assumptions, prioritizes features, and creates a realistic roadmap that maximizes the chances of shipping a polished v1.0 within the available timeline.

The platform includes:

Idea Validation
Scope Management

A clear plan and realistic scope often matter more than writing code quickly.
